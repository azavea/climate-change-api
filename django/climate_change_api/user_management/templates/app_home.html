Welcome to Azavea’s Climate Application

We combined the Global Daily Downscaled Projections (NEX-GDDP) and the Coupled Model Intercomparison Project Phase 5 (CMIP5), and two carbon scenarios (RCP 4.5 and RCP 8.5) to give you access to low temperature, high temperature and precipitation data from 1950 to 2100. You can access 22 climate indicators for over 1,000 U.S. locations via web interface or API.
